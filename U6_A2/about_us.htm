About us

Who are Celtec Games?

We create indie games that cover 5 different genres. These include action, adventure, battle, mystery and survival. Our games cover all of the major platforms which include PC/desktop, Playstaion, Xbox, Nintendo switch and mobile (IOS,Andriod)

Here at our company Celtec we believe that when developing games creativity, collaboration and a shared vision is the heart of game devlopment. Due to our belifes our team is composed of the upmost talanted indivduals that have a lot of passion about the gaming community allowing us to produce extremely high quality entertainment.

Our mission

Our end goal is to enhance the gaming experience with games that provide entertainment that inspire and help people that play become more educated. We strive to push the boundaries of what gaming is possible to do, creating worlds that are immersive for players to explore and enjoy. We commit to delivering cross platform games that resonate with audences worldwide, we make this happen by using the latest technology and trends.
